Non Technical 2019





















Activity:
Fireless cooking contest: “FLAVOUR FEAST”
SGU Knowledge Hub club at Sanjay Ghodawat University carried out a Cultural (NonTechnical) activity for the academic year 2019-20.

Activity 1: COOKING CONTEST The Non-Technical activity conducted by the Knowledge Hub Club was the “Flavour Feast” held on 12th October 2019. Under this activity, the participants were bounded by the main criteria i.e. Cooking without fire.

The participants exhibited their culinary skills and prepared nutritious and healthy dishes with fruits, vegetables, sprouts etc. The atmosphere was filled with excitement and anticipation. Attired in chef caps, the participants displayed their skill, knowledge and creativity by making delicious dishes.

Their team work was commendable as they successfully presented their dishes in an innovative manner. The dishes had been given creative names and were presented in aesthetic style.

Mr. Bhavesh Natani and Miss. Sakshi Swami as a team bagged the first position. They had prepared Crunchy Munchi, Khatta Meetha Palak, Banana granola sticks, Malai Role and Veg Sandwich. They were awarded with a University level Recognition certificate and a cash prize of Rs.700/-.

There was a surprise round for the 2nd prize, the Pictures of all the dishes presented by the participants were uploaded on the official Instagram handle of Knowledge hub club and the participants with highest likes (after 2 days) on their picture were to be awarded with the cashback of Rs. 120/-. Ms. Sharwari Pawar and Ms. Pratiksha Patil as a team stood second with the maximum number of likes on their picture.
